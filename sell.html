Selling Rules
When selling items on Trade Wave, users must ensure accurate listings with detailed descriptions and photos, including any defects or damage. It is crucial to provide transparency to potential buyers regarding the condition and specifications of the items being sold. Pricing should be clearly stated, indicating whether it is negotiable or fixed. Accepted payment methods and delivery options should be specified to avoid any confusion during the transaction process. It is recommended that buyers inspect items thoroughly before completing the transaction to avoid any misunderstandings or disputes later on. In terms of payment, sellers should clarify the accepted payment methods, whether it be cash, bank transfer, or any other preferred mode. Additionally, payment terms and deadlines, if applicable, should be clearly communicated to avoid any delays or misunderstandings. For transactions involving shipping, sellers should provide information about shipping options, costs, and estimated delivery times. It is important to set realistic expectations regarding delivery to ensure a positive buying experience. For local transactions where meet-ups are arranged, sellers should provide details about the location, date, and time of the meet-up. Clear communication and punctuality are essential during these meet-ups to ensure a smooth transaction process. Buyers and sellers should both prioritize safety and security during in-person interactions, such as meeting in public places and avoiding sharing sensitive personal information.
